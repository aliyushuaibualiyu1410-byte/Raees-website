LABORATORY INVENTORY STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE (SOP)

Document ID: SOP-LAB-INV-001 | Version 2.4 | Effective Date: June 2026

1. Purpose & Scope

This document defines strict procedures for maintaining, counting, storing, and disposing of laboratory reagents, samples, and equipment elements. Adherence ensures academic integrity, quality safety standards, and operational precision.

2. Inventory Logging Protocols

  • All incoming chemical compounds and biological agents must immediately be tracked down within this system before floor staging.
  • Required fields include the exact Chemical Abstract Service (CAS) registered registry ID where applicable.
  • Quantities must be updated immediately upon deduction or physical usage cycles within active benches.

3. Threshold & Expiry Enforcement

Items flagged under Low Stock (below minimum structural safety thresholds) must immediately trigger standard acquisition procurement queues. Any materials crossing past expiration metrics must immediately be pulled from primary cabinets and logged into historical waste cycles to prevent workflow errors.
